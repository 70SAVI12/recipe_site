Chocolate cake recipe.

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Choclate calke recipe." Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Coocking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35

Ingredients

Back Chocolate :200 g

Sugar : 150 g

Butter : 200 g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80 g

Instructions

1

Mix the flour with 4 eggs

2

Add the butter and sugar

3

Mix for 10 more minutes

4

Baking: 40 min in oven at 180 C)
